SEN's renewable energy conceptual simulation, SIREN, is used to calculate energy generation for renewable energy power stations using SAM models.

To get started press F1 (menu option Help -> Help) to view the SIREN Help file. It is strongly recommended you read the help file in detail to ensure you take advantage of the full capabilities and understand the limitations of SIREN.
